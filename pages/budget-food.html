Budget Eats & Snacks

Eating on the island does not have to drain your wallet. These ideas keep most meals under $15 per person while still giving you plenty of flavor, variety, and quick options between beach time and activities.

Under $15 Per Person

  • Tacos and combo plates
  • Pizza by the slice
  • Seafood baskets
  • Grab-and-go deli subs & fruit cups

Money Savers

  • Share sides + water pitchers
  • Look for weekday specials
  • Pack big waters for the beach

Tip: Check local boards and restaurant windows for daily specials, lunch combos, and happy hour deals. A quick photo of the menu can help you compare prices and plan ahead for your next meal.
