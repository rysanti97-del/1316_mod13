Compare the Top Beach Access Points

Whether you want easy parking, quick walk-on access, or the freedom of drive-on sand, this guide helps you choose the best option for your visit. Compare fees, amenities, and atmosphere across the island’s most popular entry points.

Beach Access Comparison
Location Fees Amenities Notes
Isla Blanca Park $ (entry) Restrooms, showers, jetties, snack stands Most convenient; great views
Andy Bowie Park $ (parking) Restrooms, showers Quieter north-end vibe
Access #3 (walk-on) Free Limited amenities Arrive early for parking
Access #6 (drive-on) Free (+ conditions) No facilities Check tides & traction
City Beach Lots Varies Near shops & food Watch signs & meters

Helpful Tips

  • Arrive early for shaded parking and cooler sand.
  • Carry small bills or a debit card for parking meters.
  • Check tide levels before accessing drive-on areas (#6).
  • Pack extra water if you choose a no-amenities beach.
