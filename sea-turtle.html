Visitor Information

Sea Turtle, Inc. is one of the most popular educational stops on South Padre Island. The center focuses on sea turtle rescue, rehabilitation, and conservation, offering exhibits that are great for families, students, and anyone interested in coastal wildlife.

Hours

Plan to spend 30–60 minutes exploring the exhibits and meeting the resident turtles.

Cost

General admission is budget-friendly. Prices may vary, so check the official website before your trip.

What to Expect

  • Rescue and rehab turtle tanks
  • Educational conservation displays
  • A family-friendly gift shop
